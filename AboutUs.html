ABOUT INFINITO TOWING SERVICES INC.

Don Regulo Aispuro has been a auto-body worker for over 40 years in bodyshops across the Los Angeles County region and Mexico. He spent most of his career working in the Hollywood area and the San Fernando Valley and has worked on cars of all makes and models. Don Rgulo's passion for the autobody industry, his diligence and hard working spirit, led him to purchase his first tow truck in 2022. Inspired by other tow trucking businesses, he invested his hard earned savings and began to operate his own autobody towing business. Housed in the San Fernando City, a hub for classic cars, Infinito Towing Sevices (ITS) takes pride in its knowledge for autobody care. ITS understand that anybody can get stuck in a bind when we least imagine it and we understand the importance of your vehicle in your daily life. You can expect detailed care and knowledge of your car with ITS. Don Regulo's knowledge in the industry and connections to autobody shops in Los Angeles and San Fernando Valley can lead you to rest assure that your car will be in in the best hands.
